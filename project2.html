Revolutionizing Cat Care with the Ultimate Cat Feeder

Background

The pet-care market lacks comprehensive health monitoring and management solutions for cats, leading cat owners to rely on veterinarians for answers to their feline-related questions. Additionally, many cat owners lack knowledge about the consequences of overfeeding and various diseases linked to it.

Problem Statement

Support cat owners’ desire and effort to track and understand their cats’ habits and health metrics while creating a safe space for their cat to eat.

Solution

An automated IoT device that measures and tracks key cat health metrics like weight, food, and water intake while limiting feeder access to authorized pets via a sensor. Cat owners can customize their experience and indicate when and how much their cat(s) should eat. Over time, the feeder collects pertinent information to show owners data on their pet’s eating and drinking habits - including changes and potential adjustments.

Challenges
  1. Market Differentiation: In a saturated market, the Ultimate Cat Feeder needs to stand out by offering more than just scheduled feeding times.
  2. Changing Habits: Overcoming the resistance to change in both owners and pets can be challenging, as many owners prefer sticking to their established routines.
  3. User Engagement: Identifying and engaging cat owners willing to participate in the study and allow monitoring of their interactions with their cats is vital for gaining a deeper understanding of the user-device relationship.

Development Process

  • 1

    Research Study & Findings

    To gain insights into cat feeding habits, owner concerns, and challenges they face, a 4-day diary study was conducted with five primary caretakers. This study explored interactions with their cats, their health concerns, and the obstacles they encountered as cat owners. It addressed questions such as the owners' approach to their cat's eating habits, their pain points and concerns, and areas where improvement and support were needed. Additionally, a survey was conducted with 39 cat owners to supplement and validate the diary study results.

  • 2

    User Enactments Study

    A speed dating matrix was developed, covering three dimensions and five scenarios, focusing on the feasibility of tasks. Selected speed dating scenarios were transformed into actionable tasks using props and subsequently tested with users.

  • 3

    System Prototype

    Based on user test feedback, a system-level design was developed, addressing user needs. Components of the finalized system proposal included a cat house with a weighted food bowl for food intake monitoring, a pet weight scale, a water level monitor for hydration tracking, and an integrated camera for recognizing authorized animals and controlling feeder access.

  • 4

    Wizard Of Oz

    Due to the complexity and cost of developing a high-fidelity prototype, we used the 'Wizard of Oz' technique to demonstrate our product concept. An IoT component was integrated to automate the device, signaling feeding times, low food levels, and unauthorized access attempts.

Impact

The Ultimate Cat Feeder offers cat owners a revolutionary solution to monitor and manage their cats' health and feeding habits. By automating key aspects of pet care, it provides peace of mind and valuable insights into cat well-being, helping to prevent overfeeding and related health issues. While challenges remain, this project represents a significant step forward in cat care technology.

Future Steps
To take "The Ultimate Cat Feeder" to the next level, we will need to incorporate the following enhancements and refinements:
  1. 1. Enhanced Physical Device Accessibility: We aim to improve the accessibility characteristics of the physical device. This will include features such as voice command integration, multiple screen options, and the incorporation of physical icons. These additions will make the device even more user-friendly and convenient for a diverse range of cat owners.
  2. 2. Adjusted Size Options: To cater to a broader audience and ensure compatibility with varying spaces and aesthetic preferences, we will introduce adjusted size options for "The Ultimate Cat Feeder." This expansion will enable consumers to choose a device that seamlessly fits into their living environment.
  3. 3. Additional Feeder Openings: In order to enhance the device's adaptability to different household configurations, we will incorporate additional feeder openings. This modification will provide greater flexibility, making it suitable for a wide range of home setups and ensuring that all cats are accommodated comfortably.
