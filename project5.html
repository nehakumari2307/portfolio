Enhancing User Experience for Choose Improve's Website

Background

Choose Improve, an initiative by Improve Studios, LLC, draws from research in improvisation, psychology, and brain science to support individuals and teams in their personal and professional well-being. The program offers a range of services, including online and in-person classes, as well as asynchronous email content, to cater to individual and group needs.

Problem Statement

To enhance the usability and accessibility of the Choose Improve website and evaluate users' overall experience while interacting with the platform.

Solution

A comprehensive report was generated, highlighting key findings at each stage of the project. It also presented a set of recommendations aimed at improving the platform's user experience. These recommendations included adjustments such as optimizing text placement, simplifying navigation for essential features, and introducing user login profiles, allowing users to set wellness goals.

Challenges
  1. 1. Audience Identification: Identifying the right target audience within the wellness domain posed a challenge during the survey study. Given the broad scope of wellness, defining and reaching the specific user base was a complex task.
  2. 2. Scope Definition: As wellness is a vast domain, narrowing down the evaluation scope for comparative analysis was crucial. Ensuring that the analysis provided valuable insights required focus within a specific domain.
  3. 3. Time Constraints: Due to time limitations, A/B testing, which could have provided a more in-depth analysis, could not be conducted on the website. Such testing would have allowed for a thorough examination of user behavior and preferences.

Development Process

  • 1

    Interviews

    One-on-one interviews were conducted with six users to gain insight into their individual experiences with the product. Additionally, a stakeholder meeting with the client was held to gather valuable insights. Information gathered from these interviews was used for qualitative theming, affinity wall analysis, persona development, and scenario creation.

  • 2

    Comparative Analysis

    A comparative analysis was conducted to identify and analyze competitors in the wellness, comedy, and educational technology markets. Seven products or services in these domains were selected for in-depth comparison. This analysis not only examined competitors' features but also identified areas where Choose Improve could improve and innovate.

  • 3

    Survey

    To obtain a broader perspective and understand the needs of the average user, a survey was constructed and distributed to a diverse target audience. The service offered by Choose Improve caters to users of all age groups, and thus, a broad and inclusive survey approach was taken.

  • 4

    Heuristic Evaluation

    A heuristic evaluation was performed based on Nielsen's 10 Usability Heuristics for User Interface Design. Nine heuristics were chosen to assess the usability of the Choose Improve website. Under each heuristic, identified issues were rated on a severity scale of 3 or higher, highlighting areas requiring immediate attention.

  • 5

    Usability Testing

    The type of usability testing adopted for Choose Improve was Summative Testing. This approach was chosen because the product was already developed. Test cases were created to validate the product against defined benchmarks, with a focus on users who were new to the website. Evaluating the platform from a novice user's perspective allowed for insights into accessibility and usability.

Impact
The efforts to elevate user experience and accessibility on the Choose Improve platform have already yielded several impactful outcomes:
  1. 1. Enhanced User Satisfaction: Users have reported an improved and more satisfying experience when interacting with the Choose Improve website. The platform's usability enhancements, guided by heuristic evaluations and user testing, have resulted in a more user-friendly environment.
  2. 2. Accessibility Improvements: By addressing accessibility concerns, the platform is now more inclusive, catering to a broader audience. Users with diverse needs and abilities can now navigate the site with greater ease, ensuring that Choose Improve's services are accessible to all.
  3. 3. Streamlined Navigation: The introduction of navigation improvements, including text placement adjustments and simplified menu structures, has made it easier for users to find essential features and content. This has reduced user frustration and increased engagement.
Future Steps
To build upon the accomplishments of this project and continue refining the Choose Improve platform, the following future steps have been identified:
  1. 1. Ensure full accessibility compliance with relevant standards, such as WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines). This will make the platform universally accessible and inclusive.
  2. 2. Develop educational resources to help users make the most of Choose Improve's services. This could include tutorials, onboarding guides, and support materials to empower users.
  3. 3. Based on competitive analysis insights, continue to enhance and innovate platform features to stay at the forefront of the wellness, comedy, and educational technology markets.
